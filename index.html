Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 2025

📍 Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to all areas within Dhaka city and surrounding districts. Deliveries outside Dhaka can be arranged on request — contact us via WhatsApp.

⏱️ Delivery Timeframes

  • In-stock items: 3–5 working days.
  • Custom orders: 7–21 working days production + delivery.
  • Large items: Our team will call to schedule.
We'll notify you via WhatsApp once your order is ready.

💰 Delivery Charges

  • Dhaka city: ৳300–৳800 based on size & location.
  • Outside Dhaka: Quoted before confirmation.
  • Orders above ৳50,000: Free delivery within Dhaka.

⚠️ Damaged in Transit

Report transit damage with photos within 24 hours. We'll arrange replacement or repair at no cost.

Return & Exchange Policy

Last updated: January 2025

✅ Eligibility

Returns accepted within 7 days if item received is significantly different from order, has a manufacturing defect, or is in unused original condition.

❌ Not Eligible

  • Custom-made or personalised items.
  • Items used, assembled incorrectly or damaged after delivery.
  • Returns after 7 days.
  • Final sale items.
Custom furniture cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

🔄 How to Return

WhatsApp us within 7 days with photos. We assess within 48 hours and arrange free pickup if eligible.

💳 Refunds

Refunds issued within 7–10 working days via original payment method. Delivery charges non-refundable unless error on our side.
